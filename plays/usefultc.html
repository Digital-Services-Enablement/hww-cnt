  • The CSPS produced a reference tool for leading and managing a team in uncertain times, using the VUCA Approach
  • The term VUCA (volatility, uncertainty, complexity and ambiguity) was coined in the United States in the 1990s to describe the world after the Cold War.
    • This approach introduces a new way of leading teams in ways that might differ from what was once considered the “norm”.
